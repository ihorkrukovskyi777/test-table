About statistics

New token listings statistics are divided into three categories:

market

Tokens entering the general market. We analyse all market tokens appearing on the DeFi marketplace and check them for scams.

FREE

Tokens going out to Lotus without premium features. For users without premium access, statistics are based on information available in the free version of our site.

PREMIUM

Tokens going out to Lotus with premium features. For users with premium access, statistics are calculated by comparing the number of tokens that passed to the «Premium» list according to our filtering protocol with the number of those that ended up in the «Deleted» table.

By choosing Premium access, you get more in-depth data, which allows you to reduce risks and increase your chances of successful investments.

Important! Please keep in mind that data is subject to change and always do your own research. We are actively working on improving statistics and reducing the number of scam tokens.
